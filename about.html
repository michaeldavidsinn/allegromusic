About Us

Founded in 2006, Allegro Music Education Centre has established itself as a premier institution dedicated exclusively to the art of piano. For nearly two decades, we have been committed to cultivating musical talent in a supportive and inspiring environment. We combine technical precision with artistic expression to help every student—from young beginners to advanced performers—reach their full potential.

Our specialized curriculum is designed not just to teach notes, but to instill a lifelong love for music. With a team of experienced and certified instructors, we guide students through national and international curriculums, preparing them for exams, competitions, and public performances with confidence.

Our Journey

2007 The Classical and Jazz Music

A Night of Allegro’s First Piano Concert

A milestone event marking Allegro Music Education Centre’s first piano concert.This inaugural concert highlights their dedication and Allegro’s commitment to nurturing true musicianship. A celebration of music, community, and new beginnings.

2008 Music for Fun

Where Joyful Melodies Meet Carefree Expression

A warm continuation of Allegro Music Education Centre’s musical journey, this second concert embraces the lighter, brighter side of piano performance. “Music for Fun” celebrates creativity without pressure — a space where students play freely, explore their personality through music, and rediscover the simple happiness of making sound.

2009 Music is My Delight

A celebration of melodies that spark joy, curiosity, and wonder

The third chapter of Allegro Music Education Centre’s concert series embraces music in its most heartfelt form. Music is My Delight showcases young pianists expressing their individuality through pieces that inspire warmth and happiness.

2010 My Favourite Song

A personal soundtrack, played from the heart

The fourth chapter of Allegro Music Education Centre’s concert series celebrates the songs that shape memories, emotions, and identity. Each performance reveals a student’s own musical story, pieces chosen not just to be played, but to be felt. A gentle reminder that music becomes most beautiful when it becomes personal.

2012 Music is My Happiness

Smiles shaped in melody

The 6th concert radiates warmth, positivity, and the pure joy of making music. Students share pieces that make them feel alive, inspired, and deeply connected to their craft. It is a celebration of the happiness music brings into their daily lives.

2013 Music For Everyone

A stage that welcomes all

A heartwarming continuation of Allegro Music Education Centre’s musical journey, the seventh concert embraces inclusivity, joy, and togetherness. Music For Everyone celebrates every learner’s voice, where beginners, intermediates, and advanced performers share one harmonious stage.Our students swept awards at major national piano competitions, solidifying Allegro's reputation for excellence.

2014 The Enchanted Melody

Where notes weave a little magic

A dreamy, atmospheric concert filled with pieces that transport the audience into a world of imagination. Students play melodies that sparkle with charm, capturing the wonder and magic of musical storytelling.

2015 In Recital: Dr. Johannes Sebastian

Where Mastery Meets Melody

A milestone moment in Allegro Music Education Centre’s artistic journey — the very first piano concert, graced by the distinguished Dr. Johannes Sebastian Nugroho.

2016 Music Feeds The Soul

Where Melodies Nourish The Heart

The 10th Piano Concert stands as a soulful celebration of music’s power to comfort, inspire, and uplift. Through expressive performances and heartfelt interpretations, Allegro Music Education Centre invites the audience into an evening where every note nurtures the spirit.

2017 Allegro Piano Recital & Examination

With Dr. Johannes Sebastian Nugroho

Returning for the second time, Dr. Johannes Sebastian Nugroho once again guides Allegro students through a meaningful journey of performance, precision, and musical growth. This special session highlights each pianist’s progress, artistry, and discipline. Celebrating not just technique, but true musicianship.

2018 Music is My Sanity

Where Melodies Quiet The Mind

In Allegro’s 12th Piano Concert, “Music is My Sanity,” students take the stage to share the pieces that have grounded, comforted, and strengthened them through their musical journey.

2019 My Favourite Song

Where melodies feel personal

The 13th Piano Concert celebrates the pieces closest to each performer’s heart. A warm, intimate showcase where students share songs that shaped their stories.

2023 London College of Music Graduation

Honouring Allegro’s DipLCM, ALCM & LLCM Graduates

After years of delay caused by the global pandemic, the long-awaited London College of Music Graduation finally took place in Bali in 2023. From 2020 to 2022, ceremonies were postponed, but dedication never stopped. This special celebration gathered Allegro’s Diploma achievers from DipLCM, ALCM, to LLCM, honouring their perseverance, discipline, and musicianship through challenging times.

2025 Royal ABRSM Graduation

Honouring Excellence Beyond Borders

A proud moment for Allegro Music Education Centre as our DipABRSM and LRSM graduates receive their prestigious recognition in Singapore. An unforgettable milestone shared with the Allegro family on international ground.
